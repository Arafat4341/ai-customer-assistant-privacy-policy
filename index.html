Privacy Policy

Muin only accesses Facebook Page messages to help business owners respond to customer inquiries. We do not collect or store personal user messages, names, or contact details unless explicitly permitted by the Page owner.

Data such as Page IDs and access tokens are stored securely to enable messaging functionality. This data is retained only while the Page is connected to Muin and deleted upon disconnection. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with restricted access.

Business owners can revoke Muin’s access to their Page at any time through Facebook settings. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Muin processes data in accordance with Meta’s Platform Terms and applicable privacy regulations.
