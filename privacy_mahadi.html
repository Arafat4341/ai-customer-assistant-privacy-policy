MahadiTech – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 18 January 2026

MahadiTech (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a simple messaging assistant used only by the page owner to manage product inquiries and respond to customers. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled by the system and how it is used.

1. No External Data Collection

This app is a BYO (Bring Your Own) integration created solely for the MahadiTech Facebook Page. It does not collect, store, or process personal data from users outside Facebook Messenger.

All messages remain within Facebook Messenger and are handled according to Facebook’s own data policies.

2. Information Automatically Provided by Facebook

When someone messages the MahadiTech Page, Facebook may provide:

  • Sender ID
  • Message content
  • Message timestamps

This information is used only to allow the page owner to reply to messages. We do not store or reuse this data outside Messenger.

3. How Information Is Used

The information received through Messenger is used only for:

  • Responding to customer questions
  • Providing product information
  • Helping the page owner manage conversations

No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.

4. Data Storage & Security

This app does not store user messages or personal data on external servers. All conversations remain within Facebook Messenger and are protected by Facebook’s security systems.

5. User Rights

Users may stop messaging the page at any time. If a user wants their Messenger conversation removed, they may delete the chat directly from their Messenger app.

6. Children’s Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if needed. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
